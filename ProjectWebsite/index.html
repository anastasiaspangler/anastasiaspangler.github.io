Psyche Inspired VR

art comes alive

Anastasia Spangler, Jakob Balkovec, Daniel Sekits, Rehaan Cherukattil

Psyche Inspired VR immerses participants in artworks (2D, 3D, and motion-based) produced by over 130 undergraduate artists in NASA's Psyche Inspired program. Participants journey through a surreal landscape, engaging with the art via dialogue and interactions, and learn about NASA's mission to the metal asteroid Psyche.

The project will be distributed for Meta Quest Summer 2026.

The web-based gallery is available at: https://psyche.ssl.berkeley.edu/galleries/artwork/
