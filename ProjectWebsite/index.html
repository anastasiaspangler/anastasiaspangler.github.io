NASA Psyche Inspired Program

Psyche Inspired VR

Art comes alive in a surreal, interactive journey to the metal world of Psyche.

Meta Quest release: Summer 2026 2D, 3D, and motion-based artworks
Explore the web gallery Project overview

Psyche Inspired VR immerses participants in artworks produced by over 130 undergraduate artists in NASA's Psyche Inspired program. Visitors journey through a surreal landscape, engage the art via dialogue and interactions, and learn about NASA's mission to the metal asteroid Psyche.
